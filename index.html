What Decco is

Decco is a distributed gallery built into everyday spaces and places — where art is discovered in context, not under fluorescent lights or endless scrolling.

For Artists

Real-world distribution with curated placements that help your work get seen — and sold — naturally.

  • Your work is seen in context
  • Simple intake and placement process
  • Clear path for buyers to inquire and collect

For Hosts

Upgrade your space with curated art — with minimal effort and no awkward sales handling.

  • Decco curates and rotates pieces
  • No staffing required
  • A more beautiful space for guests

For Buyers

Discover art organically and take the next step in seconds.

  • See it in a real space
  • Scan → View → Collect
  • Support artists directly

How it works

A simple, curated process — designed for artists, hosts, and buyers.

1

Artists apply

Share your portfolio and a few details. Decco reviews for quality, fit, and placement readiness.

2

Spaces host

We partner with design-forward spaces — rentals, hospitality, studios, and select retail.

3

Buyers discover & collect

They see the work in context, scan to view details, and request to collect.
