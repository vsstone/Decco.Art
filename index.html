What Decco is

Decco is a curated art network that places artwork into real, lived-in environments — homes, hospitality, and thoughtfully designed public spaces — where people naturally connect with art.

We believe art shouldn’t live only in galleries or pop-ups. It should live on walls where life is already happening — and sometimes it should move through markets and events.

Trusted & Transparent

  • Artists retain ownership of all work
  • Insurance is optional and artist-controlled
  • Clear retrieval & rotation rules
  • Spaces have no cost; artists set prices
  • Simple, consistent payouts
