What Decco is

Decco places curated artwork into real environments — homes, hospitality, and design-forward public spaces — where people naturally connect with it.

For Artists

Distribution without the overhead. Your work is seen in context — where people can imagine it in their own life.

  • Keep ownership & pricing control
  • Real-world discovery (not endless scrolling)
  • Simple QR-based viewing + inquiry

For Spaces

Upgrade your walls with curated art. No staffing. No awkward sales handling. Upside only.

  • Rotations & refreshes as Decco grows
  • No cost to host
  • Optional revenue share when work sells

For Buyers

Discover art organically, without pressure. View the story, details, and next steps in seconds.

  • See it in a real space
  • Scan → View → Collect
  • Support artists directly
